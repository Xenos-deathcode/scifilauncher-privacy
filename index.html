SciFiLauncher – Xenos Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026

SciFiLauncher – Xenos (“the app”) is developed and maintained by Xenos (“we”, “our”, “us”). If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: cedalstar@gmail.com.

1. Information we access

The app runs on your device and does not create an account or ask you to enter personal details. It accesses certain system features only to provide launcher and assistant functionality:

Microphone / voice

SciFiLauncher – Xenos may access your device’s microphone to let you talk to the in‑app assistant and control features by voice. Audio is used only to perform your requested actions and is not stored on our servers.

Camera and video

The app may open or use the camera or video functions when you choose features that require them (for example, shortcuts that launch the system camera). The app itself does not record, store, or upload your photos or videos to our servers.

Notifications

The app may request notification access to read app names, titles, and text of your notifications so it can display or read them inside the launcher and allow you to interact with them. This information is processed on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do not operate our own backend servers for SciFiLauncher – Xenos and we do not collect or store your personal data outside your device.

2. How we use the information

We use the permissions listed above only to:

  • show and manage your apps and notifications;
  • enable voice control and assistant features;
  • provide shortcuts to camera and other system functions.

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. Third‑party services

At this time, SciFiLauncher – Xenos does not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If we add any third‑party services in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy to list them and link to their own privacy policies.

4. Children’s privacy

SciFiLauncher – Xenos is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at cedalstar@gmail.com so we can delete it.

5. Your choices and controls

You can control what the app can access at any time using your device settings:

  • You can revoke microphone, camera, and notification access in your Android permission settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, which removes it and its local data from your device.

Some features of SciFiLauncher – Xenos may not work correctly if you disable certain permissions, but the app can still be used as a basic launcher.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. If there are major changes, we may also show a notice inside the app or on the app listing.
